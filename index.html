This project presents a comprehensive multi-page business intelligence dashboard built in Microsoft Power BI for Arrakeen Electronics, a fictional global electronics retailer operating across 8 countries and 66 physical store locations with an active online sales channel. The dashboard was designed to answer real-world business questions across five strategic domains: executive performance, store operations, product and brand analytics, customer behavior, online vs. in-store channel strategy, and delivery operations.

The project demonstrates end-to-end data analysis skills including data modeling, DAX measure development, time intelligence, calculated tables, conditional formatting, and interactive report design all applied to a realistic multi-table retail dataset spanning nearly five years of transactional data.
